My Neighborhood: Downtown Detroit

About Downtown Detroit

Downtown is a neighborhood in Detroit, Michigan with a population of 7,035. Downtown is in Wayne County and is one of the best places to live in Michigan. Living in Downtown offers residents a dense urban feel and most residents rent their homes. In Downtown there are a lot of bars, restaurants, coffee shops, and parks. Many young professionals live in Downtown and residents tend to be liberal.
